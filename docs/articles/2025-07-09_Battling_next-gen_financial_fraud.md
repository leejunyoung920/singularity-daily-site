# Battling next-gen financial fraud

**출처:** https://www.technologyreview.com/2025/07/08/1119039/battling-next-gen-financial-fraud/

## 요약
1. The article discusses the growing threat of financial fraud enabled by the use of AI and other advanced technologies.

2. Criminals are using tools like voice over internet protocol, synthetic identity fraud, and credential-stuffing software to scale up and refine their attacks, making them more sophisticated and difficult to detect.

3. Synthetic identity fraud, which involves creating "Frankenstein IDs" using stolen personal data, is now costing banks $6 billion a year and is the fastest-growing financial crime in the US.

4. Fraudsters can use AI tools to identify and target victims at a much higher rate and lower cost, and can carry out millions of simultaneous digital conversations.

5. The proliferation of these technologies has civil liberties advocates concerned, as the government vows to expand surveillance of protesters and students.
