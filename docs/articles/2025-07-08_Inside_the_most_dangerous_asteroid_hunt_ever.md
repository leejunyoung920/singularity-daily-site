# Inside the most dangerous asteroid hunt ever

**출처:** https://www.technologyreview.com/2025/07/08/1119757/asteroid-hunt-2024-yr4-earth-planet-protection/

## 요약
1. A 130-300 foot long asteroid, named 2024 YR4, was detected to have a 3.1% chance of crashing into Earth in 2032, which was the highest probability for an asteroid of such dangerous dimensions.

2. The potential impact locations included densely populated cities like Mumbai, Lagos, and Bogotá, with the possibility of severe damage or total ruin.

3. Scientists and engineers around the world mobilized to find ways to stop the asteroid, considering options like ramming a spacecraft into it or using nuclear weapons.

4. Despite the high stakes and urgent situation, the planetary defense researchers remained calm and focused on their task of tracking and monitoring the asteroid.

5. Ultimately, the danger was declared to have passed, as the asteroid trackers were able to determine that it would miss Earth, demonstrating the effectiveness of the global astronomical community in addressing such a threat.
