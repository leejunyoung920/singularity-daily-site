# The Download: hunting an asteroid, and unlocking the human mind

**출처:** https://www.technologyreview.com/2025/07/08/1119840/the-download-hunting-an-asteroid-and-unlocking-the-human-mind/

## 요약
1. Astronomers faced a 3.1% chance of a 130- to 300-foot-long asteroid crashing into Earth in 2032, which was the highest risk ever detected for such a large asteroid.

2. The global network of astronomers worked quickly to track, map, and plan for the potential impact of this dangerous asteroid, before ultimately determining that it would miss Earth.

3. Despite the differences between artificial neural networks and human brains, neuroscientists believe that building brain-like neural networks is a promising path for understanding the human mind.

4. Recent studies have showcased the use of neural networks to predict human and animal behavior in psychological experiments, but explaining the underlying mechanisms remains a challenge.

5. The development of fusion energy technology has significant geopolitical implications, and the nation that leads in this field will control the benefits of this energy source.
