# Scientists Launch Moonshot to Build an Entire Human Genome From Scratch

**출처:** https://singularityhub.com/2025/07/07/scientists-launch-moonshot-to-build-an-entire-human-genome-from-scratch/

## 요약
1. The Synthetic Human Genome Project (SynHG) aims to build an entire human genome from scratch, which could lead to a second revolution in genetics.

2. The project is funded by the Wellcome Trust, a major medical charity, with £10 million ($13.6 million) in initial funding.

3. The project will be led by Jason Chin from the University of Oxford, who believes that the ability to synthesize large genomes, including human cells, could transform our understanding of genome biology and alter the horizons of biotechnology and medicine.

4. The project builds on recent advances in DNA synthesis, such as the synthesis of the entire genome of the bacteria E. coli in 2019 and the completion of the first synthetic genome of yeast in 2023.

5. The Human Genome Project, which mapped the entirety of human DNA, took thousands of researchers 13 years and nearly $3 billion, but the benefits have been enormous, including revolutionizing our understanding of the genetic basis of disease and driving rapid advances in DNA sequencing technology.
