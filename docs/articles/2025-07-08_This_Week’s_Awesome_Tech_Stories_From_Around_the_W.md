# This Week’s Awesome Tech Stories From Around the Web (Through July 5)

## 요약
1. Large language models (LLMs) are improving exponentially, with their capabilities doubling every seven months. By 2030, the most advanced LLMs should be able to complete a software-based task that takes humans a full month of work with 50% reliability, and they may be able to do these tasks much faster than humans.

2. Amazon has deployed more than one million robots in its warehouses, nearing the count of human workers, as the company has spent years automating tasks previously done by humans.

3. Experimental gene therapy has been successful in restoring hearing in a deaf teenager and a 24-year-old, with the majority of patients gaining some hearing just one month after the treatment, and all 10 showing considerable hearing improvement six months later.

4. The advancement of LLMs and the increasing automation of tasks in Amazon's warehouses highlight the rapid technological progress in various domains, with potential implications for the workforce and the way tasks are performed.

5. The success of the experimental gene therapy for hearing loss provides hope for those with genetic hearing impairments, indicating the potential of gene therapy to address various medical conditions.
